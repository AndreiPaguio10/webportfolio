Hi, I’m Andrei M. Paguio.

I’m a licensed Electronics Engineer who’s really passionate about building websites and turning ideas into something people can actually use and enjoy. I’ve been working with HTML, CSS, and Bootstrap, and I use Git to keep my projects organized and track my progress as I build.

I enjoy the whole process of web development—from starting with a simple idea to bringing it to life with clean and responsive design. There’s something satisfying about seeing a project go from a blank page to a fully working website, and I like focusing on the details that make it look polished and easy to use.

Coming from an engineering background, I approach things in a logical and structured way and I’m comfortable solving problems along the way. Right now, I’m focused on growing my skills and working toward becoming a full stack developer, always looking to learn more and take on new projects to improve.
